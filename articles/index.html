#5 Eivind Hovig - Bioinformatics in the Nordic countries - NCFH 2024 - NTNU | HUNT Cloud

#4 Marc Vaudel - Running a digital research lab with sensitive data - NCFH 2024 - NTNU | HUNT Cloud

This is a conversation with Marc Vaudel about running digital research environments for sensitive data. Marc Vaudel oversees digital labs from the University of Bergen and Norwegian Institute of Public Health that bring together scientists who use data from large cohorts and health registries in combination with large-scale biomedical data. Here he shares his perspectives on how building a culture that takes good care of the data from research participants is key to long-term scientific success.
