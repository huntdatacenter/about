"Trust is the most valuable asset in data-driven science."

Qussay is our Chief Compliance Officer at HUNT Cloud. He works with engineers and researchers to translate regulatory requirements into practical solutions that supports safe, responsible, and efficient research.

Qussay is responsible for HUNT Cloud's compliance program that enables secure access to sensitive data. This includes the oversight of our third-party verified ISO certificates for quality management (ISO 9001 ), information security management (ISO/IEC 27001) and information privacy management (ISO/IEC 27701). 

Qussay also manages our internal review program, coordinates partner audits with our data controllers, and monitors compliance with requirements in EHDS, GDPR, HIPAA and relevant national regulations such as NORMEN.

He holds a Bachelor's degree in data engineering and a Master's degree in management systems, and has worked on compliance at HUNT Cloud since 2018.
